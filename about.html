About the suite

PDF Merge Pro – All-in-One File Tool

PDF Merge Pro is not just for merging PDFs, but also for converting and compressing files. Use one clean workspace to merge multiple PDFs, convert images between JPG, PNG, and WEBP, and compress images for faster sharing and storage.

Features

  • Merge multiple PDFs into a single file
  • Convert JPG, PNG, and WEBP with instant results
  • Compress images efficiently without extra tools
  • Client-side processing with zero uploads

How It Works

  1. Upload your files
  2. Choose the action (merge, convert, or compress)
  3. Download the result instantly

Privacy & Security

Your files never leave your device. All processing happens directly in your browser, which keeps your documents and images private and secure.
